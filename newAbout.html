About



I have always loved the arts. As a child, I used to draw maps leading to hidden treasures and elaborate maps of hidden cities in the trees. In high school, I took every studio art class available, while simultaenously diving into the world of theater costume. In college, I headed towards technology and programming, though never quite lost sight of my dream to design. After graduating, I began to explore fashion, creating my own pieces while learning about textiles and dyeing techniques. Now, I hope to intergrate art and creativity into my career. I hope to use my wide range of experiences to create projects that make people re-think what they consider to be normal, as I continue to push my own understanding of what surrounds me.

Born in Paris, raised in America, I have lived my life in between borders. Alongside my love for all things creative, I am passionate about language and culture. I have traveled throughout the globe, living in India and Japan for extended periods of time. I currently reside in Tokyo. I am bilingual in French and English and speak Japanese at a professional level.
