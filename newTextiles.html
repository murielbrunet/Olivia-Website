Textiles

In the spring of 2015, I was fortunate enough to spend a month studying kimono arts in the small city of Tokamachi in Niigata, Japan. Working with three different master artisans, I learned about various techniques from first hand experience used to weave and dye fabrics to create Tokamachi's famous kimonos. From there, I developed a deep love for all things fabric and began making my own clothing.
